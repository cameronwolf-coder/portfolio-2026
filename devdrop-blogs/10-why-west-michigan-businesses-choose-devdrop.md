## Industries We Serve Across West Michigan

One of the benefits of 15 years and 500-plus projects is that we've built networks for just about every type of business you can think of. Here are some of the industries where we do our most frequent work.

### Manufacturing and Warehousing

Factory floors and warehouses are tough environments for networks. Concrete walls, metal structures, heavy machinery causing interference, extreme temperatures, and dust. We design ruggedized networks that hold up in these conditions -- reliable wireless coverage on the production floor, wired connections for workstations and PLCs, and security cameras that cover yards, loading docks, and entry points.

### Healthcare and Dental

Healthcare networks have to be reliable and secure. Patient data, electronic health records, imaging systems, and connected medical devices all depend on a network that's fast, stable, and HIPAA-compliant. We build networks with the security and segmentation that healthcare environments require, and we make sure everything is documented for compliance audits.

### Commercial Real Estate

Property managers need infrastructure that works for every tenant. We handle structured cabling for entire buildings, tenant buildouts, building-wide wireless, and security camera systems. Whether you're managing a single office building or a portfolio of commercial properties, we design systems that are flexible enough to adapt as tenants change.

### Hospitality and Retail

Hotels, restaurants, and retail stores need guest WiFi that works, POS systems that stay connected, and security cameras that cover every angle. We design networks that handle high device density, separate guest traffic from business traffic, and keep everything secure.

### Construction and Trades

Construction companies and trade shops need connectivity at job sites, fabrication shops, and equipment yards. We provide both temporary and permanent wireless solutions, point-to-point links for outdoor coverage, and security cameras for sites that need monitoring.

### Professional Services

Law firms, accounting firms, financial advisors, and other professional offices need networks where downtime simply isn't an option. We build redundant, high-performance networks that keep your team connected and your client data secure.

### Nonprofits and Community Organizations

Nonprofits often have tight budgets and big missions. We work with community organizations to deliver right-sized solutions that respect your budget without cutting corners on quality. Good infrastructure shouldn't be a luxury.
